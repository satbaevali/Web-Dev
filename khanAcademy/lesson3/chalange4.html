Pizza Pepperoni

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Pizza dough 1 base
Tomato sauce 1/2 cup
Mozzarella cheese 1 cup (shredded)
Pepperoni slices 10-15 slices
Olive oil 1 tbsp

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 220°C (425°F).
Step 2: Roll out the pizza dough on a baking tray.
Step 3: Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the base.
Step 4: Sprinkle the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce.
Step 5: Arrange the pepperoni slices on the cheese.
Step 6: Drizzle olive oil over the pizza.
Step 7: Bake in the oven for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbling.
Step 8: Slice and serve hot.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 25 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Bacon 150g
Eggs 3
Parmesan Cheese 50g
Black Pepper To taste

Step 1: Boil the spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water.

Step 2: Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy.

Step 3: Whisk eggs with grated Parmesan and black pepper in a bowl.

Step 4: Combine spaghetti, bacon, and egg mixture, adding pasta water as needed for creaminess.
